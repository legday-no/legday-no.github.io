Acroyoga

Play, challenge, connect

Acroyoga is a yoga variant which draws inspiration from traditional yoga practices, acrobatics, and Thai massage. The combination of these allows us to build strength, flexibility, and stability while having a lot of fun. You practice with one or two friends, depending on the experience and challenge level of the movements.

Challenge your body and mind in a safe space. You'll learn to do things and move in ways you never thought possible.

Improve your strength, flexibility, and stability in a safe environment.

We host courses, workshops, and offer private training for individuals and groups alike. Contact us to hear what we can offer to invite you to an active and playful experience.

Håvard graduated as an Acroyoga® certified teacher in the winter of 2021, and has been practicing actively since 2020.
