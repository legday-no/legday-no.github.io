Acroyoga is a yoga variant which draws inspiration from traditional yoga practices, acrobatics, and Thai massage. The combination of these allows us to build strength, flexibility, and stability while having a lot of fun. You practice with one or two friends, depending on the experience and challenge level of the movements.
