Acroyoga is a yoga variant which draws inspiration from traditional yoga practices, acrobatics, and Thai massage. The combination of these allows us to build strength, flexibility, and stability while having a lot of fun. You practice with one or two friends, depending on the experience and challenge level of the movements.

For classes, events, or personal training, please get in touch via email at acro@legday.no.

Let's stand UP - AcroYoga workshop for beginners/intermediate

schedule Sun 17 Oct 2021
place Belgium, Brussels

For three hours, you’ll be entertained and challenged by basic and improver techniques and progressions for STANDING acrobatics leading to new, creative and fun static poses and transitions. Expect to stand on shoulders, forearms, knees, and hands – and more! We guarantee there will be something new and crazy fun for everyone! We will also demonstrate safe spotting techniques and will ensure everyone is able to take their own pace to stay happy and involved.
